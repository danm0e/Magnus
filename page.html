The benefits of private jet charter to business and profitability - whether it’s cost effectively carrying senior executives around an enterprise, or supporting entrepreneurial opportunities with face to face sales meetings are becoming more and more valued and utilised by dynamic organisations. Magnus Aviation understands these benefits and aims to enable the use of charter by more businesses by making the process simple to procure, flexible and by providing charter flights within a complete travel services context that includes travel consultancy, travel planning, special contract rates, innovative ‘ways to fly’ and automated billing systems for multi-charter clients. Magnus Aviation is a member of The Baltic Air Charter Association (BACA) and adheres to the BACA professional guidelines for industry best practice, compliance and corporate governance.

Chartering with Magnus Aviation will deliver all the benefits of business or private jet charter

  • Luxury and comfort
  • Discretion and security
  • Create your schedule
  • Access to more airports
  • Private terminal access
  • Global coverage

In addition by choosing Magnus Aviation you will further benefit from

  • Cost efficiency: our insight, knowledge, buying power and reputation means we can find you the best private jet charter prices, ensuring you always receive the most cost effective solution.
  • Choice of aircraft: with access to up to 50,000 aircraft from 130 different types, we will always be able to source the right ‘plane for your charter requirements.
  • Personal account manager: your dedicated charter expert is available 24/7 to assist you with any requirements you may have, from selecting menus for in-flight cuisine to making last minute changes to your arrangements.

Chartering with Magnus Aviation is good for value for your business

We provide aircraft charters to business professionals, to maximise their time efficiency.Chartering private jets allows executives and senior management to fulfill tight schedules and make multiple meetings in different cities or carry out several office location visits in a single day. Travelling by private jet is the most comfortable, safe and secure way of flying your company’s senior executives. It allows for private pre-meetings or briefings and means passengers arrive fresh and ready to do business.

Chartering with Magnus Aviation ensures the height of personal service, discretion and flexibility

Private jets are also used for leisure purposes, allowing you, your family and friends to travel where and when they want in total comfort and privacy. Whether it is a short European flight for a weekend’s skiing, or a two-week holiday in the tropics, we will always find the most suitable private jet to make sure that you and your party arrives safely and with the minimum of fuss. Access to private terminals means avoiding the crowds at busy airports and long queues through security screening and boarding.
