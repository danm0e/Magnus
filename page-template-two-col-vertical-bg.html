Our Story

Committed to private and business clients

Magnus Aviation was launched in 2013 by father and daughter team Jodi & Mark Fisk and Guy Richardson. Guy and Mark already ran a successful business which they started in 2011 and looked jointly to expand in a different direction so weapproached Jodi to set up an aviation side of the business. Through insights gained during her private aviation experience, Jodi had long felt that there was ‘a space in the charter broking market for a niche business that really understands the business and passenger needs of corporate customers as well as matching their commercial requirements.’

Along with Mark and Guy, Jodi set about putting together a boutique team designed to seize this opportunity. Both Mark Fisk and Guy Richardson are experienced businessmen and entrepreneurs, bringing to the table their indepth knowledge and experience of both the strategic and day-to-day management of a successful service business. Joining Jodi as Head of Charter, both Managing Director’s Mark and Guy, the team also includes Francesca Garnett, Head of Operations. The business model is service-led, scaleable and has cornerstones set around the needs of the business charter client. This is Magnus Aviation’s key point of difference and the rationale for this winning approach is backed-up by proven testimonials and positive feedback from client panels.’

The future development plans for Magnus Aviation include organic growth of the company’s charter business with expansion of the client facing and flight management teams. This plan will also mean the resourcing of team members with specialisms - over and above their charter experience - enabling additional and complementary services to be offered to clients. For example, a dedicated executive airliner division, the charter of cargo aircraft or the provision of event management services. In the medium to long-term Magnus Aviation will set up regional branches at other UK business centres and charter transport hubs as well as overseas.

Magnus Aviation is your complete corporate charter travel provider. We can effectively act as your company’s outsourced travel department, providing a full service. From a one-off senior executive flight, or travel to regular client meetings or trade show tours, we can help. Entering into long-term strategic business partnerships where we cater for all your enterprise charter and associated travel requirements.

In addition to the arranging of the charter flight or flights, Magnus Aviation will put together a seamless service package for your company including: strategic travel and itinerary planning, arranging local transfers, specialist catering, accommodation booking, handling of cargo, management of travel document requirements and courier services. The complete before, during and after flight service.

One call to our team allows you to relax in the knowledge that your personal specialist account manager will ensure that the best possible outcome is delivered for your passengers and your business. Once we embark on a full service relationship our bespoke CRM system will ensure the highest standards in customer service are met. It will provide communications continuity, flight status and financial reporting, enabling cost savings to be realised where identified. Some of the areas we can help you with your travel solutions include:

Corporate group charter, Chauffeur and car services, Visa information, Flight tickets and Hotel bookings.

Our experience in the charter business will deliver the best experience for your business charter. To find out more about our business jet travel solutions please call our charter team.
